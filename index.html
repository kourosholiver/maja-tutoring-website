✦ About Maja

A teacher who makes you feel seen 💜

Hi, I'm Maja! 👋 I'm a Swedish actress and passionate teacher, and I believe learning is one of the most beautiful adventures life has to offer — when you do it with the right person.

I grew up completely surrounded by the world of opera — both of my parents are professional opera singers. Music, theatre, and the magic of performance have been woven into my life since the very beginning, and that love pours straight into everything I teach.

I struggled in school myself as a child, so I know exactly what it feels like when learning is hard. That's why everything I do is built on patience, curiosity, zero judgment, and genuine, overflowing joy. I don't just teach — I discover what makes you tick, and we build from there, together. ✨

"Every single student deserves to feel like the most important person in the room. Because when you feel truly heard and seen, that's when magic happens."
🌞 Warm & Nurturing 🌿 Infinitely Patient 💖 Playful & Silly ✨ Deeply Creative 🎭 Actress & Performer 🌱 Always Encouraging

The secret to learning isn't discipline or pressure — it's joy. My teaching begins by genuinely getting to know you: what excites you, what you dream about, what makes you laugh until your sides hurt.

I've spent my whole life learning how to make people feel at ease. Kids, adults, nervous beginners — everyone gets the same thing from me: unconditional warmth and the deep, unshakeable belief that they can do this. 💛
